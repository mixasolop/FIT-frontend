Instructions

1. Upload a Music File

Click or drag-and-drop on the dashed upload icon to select a mono-instrumental file (MP3, WAV).

2. Convert to Notes

After choosing your file, click the green “Convert to Notes” button. Watch the loading bars until the transcription completes.

3. Real-time Recording

Or click “Start Recording” to capture live audio from your mic. When you’re done, hit “Stop Recording” to process.

4. Download & Print

Once your notes appear, use the built-in audio player to preview, then download or print your sheet music.

Supported Formats

  • Files: MP3, WAV (mono only)
  • Browser Recording: Live capture via your device microphone

Troubleshooting

If transcription fails or results look off, make sure:

  • Your audio is truly mono (not stereo).
  • Your browser has permission to access your mic.
  • You refresh the page and try again if something hangs.
