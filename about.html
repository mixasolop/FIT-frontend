About Us

Who We Are

We’re eight second-semester CS students from Warsaw University of Technology, united by our love of music. For our FIT project, we set out to solve a common problem: finding sheet music for your favorite songs.

Our Mission

Make music learning easy and fun—no more hunting for tabs. Just upload or record a mono track and get accurate sheet music in seconds.

How It Works

  • Upload/Record: MP3, WAV, FLAC, or live browser recording
  • Analyze: Spectrogram-based noise removal and note matching
  • Render: Instant, editable sheet music you can download or print

Why Music2Notes

  • Instant Access – No more hunting for tabs or transcriptions online.
  • Intuitive UI – A clean, straightforward interface means anyone can jump right in.
  • Future-Proof – Our modular pipeline (spectrogram → note detection → rendering) lets us swap in better models and codecs as they emerge.

Don’t let missing sheet music hold you back. With Music2Notes, the notes to your favorite melodies are always at your fingertips—playing the songs you love has never been easier.
